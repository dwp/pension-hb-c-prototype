Apply for Pension Credit and Housing Benefit

If you are of State Pension age, you can use this service to apply for both Pension Credit and Housing Benefit at the same time.

Pension Credit can top up your income to help with living costs, and Housing Benefit can help you pay your rent.

You can use this service to check if you are eligible for both benefits. You may only be eligible for one, or neither.

Applying for Pension Credit

You can use this service to apply for Pension Credit if you:

  • are resident in Great Britain
  • have appropriate immigration status

You should not use this service to apply for Pension Credit if you are:

  • below State Pension age (you should check if you can apply for Universal Credit instead)
  • currently away from Great Britain for more than 4 weeks, unless it is for medical treatment or recovery
  • already outside Great Britain
  • moving away from Great Britain permanently

Applying for Housing Benefit

You can use this service to apply for Housing Benefit if you:

  • are resident in Great Britain
  • either rent the home that you live in, or a private room in it

You can not use this service to apply for Housing Benefit if you:

  • are part of a mixed age couple, where one of you is not of pension age (you should check if you can apply for Universal Credit instead)
  • below State Pension age (you should check if you can apply for Universal Credit instead)
  • have £16,000 or more in savings, investments, or other assets unless you get the Guarantee Credit part of Pension Credit

If you are only eligible for one of the benefits

You can only use this service if you are eligible for both of the benefits.

If you are only eligible to apply for Pension Credit, you should use the Apply for Pension Credit online service.

If you are only eligible to apply for Housing Benefit, you should apply through your Local Authority.

Other help you may be entitled to

If you are eligible for Pension Credit you can also get other help, such as:

  • Cold Weather Payments (or Winter Heating Payments in Scotland)
  • a Council Tax Reduction
  • a free TV licence if you’re aged 75 or over
  • help with NHS dental treatment, glasses and transport costs for hospital appointments, if you get the Guarantee Credit part of Pension Credit
  • help with your heating costs through the Warm Home Discount Scheme

Information you will need

You will need to tell us about:

  • your immigration status
  • any time spent outside Great Britain in the last 3 months
  • your living situation, including any landlord or agency details
  • information about any other people living with you
  • your address history for the last 3 months
  • your income, money and investments
  • any property or land you own
