What happens next

We will write to you with our decision about your Pension Credit application.

Your local authority will contact you with their decision about your Housing Benefit application.

Your Pension Credit application

We may need more information to process your application.

If your application is successful, we will pay your Pension Credit into the same account as your State Pension.

You will be able to change this to a different account once your Pension Credit is in payment.

Your Housing Benefit application

If your local authority needs more information to process your application, they may call you if you have given them a telephone number. Otherwise they will write to you.
